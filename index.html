Brief intro

I’m a tech and science enthusiast. Professionally, my activity covers system administration and DevOps. My main interests are *NIX environments, the infrastructure built around them, cloud computing and information security. I’ve been involved in research activities within the field of quantum information, which led to achievements including publications in international journals and conference proceedings.

Background, interests

I wrote both my Bachelor and Master theses on quantum information science topics, initiatives in which I’ve invested a great deal of dedication and time for several years. These have been the highlights for me as a student, personal endeavors I started and fostered alongside a few dedicated professors. I worked on the theoretical side and I implemented some results using IBM’s quantum computing framework, Qiskit.

I’m familiar with other major areas in physics. It’s all out of passion and now I rarely get back to it, but I’ll gladly have more or less formal/specific conversations on any subject. Other areas I’ve explored in more detail are philosophy of mind and history of the twentieth century.
